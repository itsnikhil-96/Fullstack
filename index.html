Samsung Galaxy S24

Samsung Galaxy S24 mobile was launched on 17th January 2024. The phone comes with a 120 Hz refresh rate 6.20-inch touchscreen display (FHD+). The display sports Gorilla Glass for protection. Samsung Galaxy S24 is powered by an octa-core processor. It comes with 8GB of RAM. The Samsung Galaxy S24 runs Android 14 and is powered by a 4000mAh non-removable battery.
